My Favorite Recipes

White Sauce Pasta

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Cheese
  • Vegetables (optional)

Instructions:

Cook pasta, prepare white sauce with butter, flour, and milk. Mix with pasta and vegetables. Top with cheese.

Pepperoni Garlic Bread

Ingredients:

  • Baguette or bread
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Cheese

Instructions:

Spread garlic butter on bread, add pepperoni and cheese. Bake until golden and crispy.

Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients:

  • Paneer cubes
  • Butter
  • Tomato puree
  • Cream
  • Spices (garam masala, chili powder)

Instructions:

Cook paneer in a rich tomato-based gravy with butter, cream, and spices. Serve hot with naan or rice.

Fruit Salad with Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • Mixed fruits (apple, banana, grapes, etc.)
  • Honey or sugar syrup
  • Ice cream (vanilla or your choice)
  • Nuts (optional)

Instructions:

Chop fruits, mix with honey or syrup. Serve chilled with a scoop of ice cream on top.
